Amphawa Thai Noodle house

Site purpose

This site provides information about a Thai restaurant, including their story, menu, location, and ways to contact them for the clients to be able to enjoy of a vast diversity of asian food in this case thailandese

Scenarios

  • What are the most popular dishes served?
  • Where can I find their location?

Color schema:

The color schema I chose is are Orange and yellow accompanaied by the white and black color defaults, the brown for the header, navigation bar, footer and button of submit in the forms, orange for a caroussel background color and mobile navigation drop down, white for the entire background color of the body of the site and black for all the texts, links and text in buttons

Typography:

I chose 2 fonts for my project, for Titles and subheadings Fira Sans and for regular text IBM Plex Sans
