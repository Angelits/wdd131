Who We Are

Amphawa Thai Noodle House is a restaurant located in the Richmond District in San Francisco, it opened in 2011 and has been in the business since, it was nominated Top Thai in the bay Awarded by San Francisco Chronicle - August 2013, January 2021, January 2022, May 2023, and February 2025, they offer a great variety of thai style food as Stir-fied noodles, soups, fried rice etc...

The Chef

The Chef and Owner Kob Varaphols is an award-winning chef who brings 20+ years of Thai culinary tradition and highest quality work in the restaurant industry his hopes and goals are to provide authentic food and for the clients to enjoy and have an exceptional experience
