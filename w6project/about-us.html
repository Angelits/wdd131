About us

Amphawa Thai Noodle House was founded with one goal in mind: to share authentic Thai street food and long timed traditional flavors with our community in the Bay Area. From the aromatic broths to our handcrafted curry dishes, everything is prepared with love, tradition, and the freshest ingredients.

Why 'Amphawa'?

Amphawa is a small, charming riverside town in Thailand known for its floating market and cozy food stalls. The name is a tribute to its culinary spirit and welcoming vibe — that’s what we like to bring to the table!
